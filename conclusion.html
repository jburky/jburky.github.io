Conclusion

The CMR system that monitors communities for medical emergencies and dispatches medical professionals efficiently has a lot of Big Data challenges. We reviewed different methods and technologies that would be able to support the volume, velocity, and variance challenges faced with our CMR system. Although more advancement implementations would be required to fully support Dr. Brown and the CMR system there are technologies that are headed in the right direction.
